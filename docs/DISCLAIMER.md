diagnose, treat, or provide clinical decision support, and it is not intended
for use in making clinical decisions.

EHR's export and validated by automated QA, but *you* must review them for
completeness and accuracy before clinical, legal, or billing use. A
reconstruction is only as complete as the export it came from — and vendors
have been known to omit data from exports.

**Retention is your job.** State medical-record retention requirements range

required), but storing, backing up, and protecting it is the operator's
responsibility.

**Your accounts, your rules.** Delivery features operate against EHR accounts

with your vendor agreements. This tool never bypasses authentication.
